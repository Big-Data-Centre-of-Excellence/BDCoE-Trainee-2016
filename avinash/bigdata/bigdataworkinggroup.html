Big Data Working Group

Introduction to the Big Data Working Group


The Big Data Working Group (BDWG) will be identifying scalable techniques for data-centric security and privacy problems. BDWG's investigation is expected to lead to crystallization of best practices for security and privacy in big data, help industry and government on adoption of best practices, establish liaisons with other organizations in order to coordinate the development of big data security and privacy standards, and accelerate the adoption of novel research aimed to address security and privacy issues.

The working group will also put together research proposals for joint funding by government and industry initiatives.
