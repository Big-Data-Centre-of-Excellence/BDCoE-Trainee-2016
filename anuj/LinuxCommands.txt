1.ls:                           list all the files in current directory.
2.pwd:                       present working directory.
3.cd:                          change directory.
4.clear:                      to clear the screen.
5.mkdir:                     make directory.
6.cat:                          to view or edit.
7.man <command>:  to read manual for a particular command.
8.rm:                          remove file.
9.touch filename:      create file.
10.tail filename:        display last 10 lines of the particular file.
11.head filename:      display first 10 lines of a particular file.
