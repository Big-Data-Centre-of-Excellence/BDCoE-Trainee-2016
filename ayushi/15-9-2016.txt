Topic:JAVA-Abstract Window Toolkit(AWT).

Content:
AWT is huge.It consist of 12 packages and two of the 
most common are-
a)java.awt package contains
     i)Component classes.
     ii)Container classes.
     iii)Layout managers.
     iv)Custom Graphics classes.
b)java.awt.event package support event handling.
      i)Event classes.
      ii)Event Listener.
      iii)Event Listener Interface.


AWT Classes-
a)AWTEvent-encapsulates AWT event.
b)BorderLayout-The border layout manager.
c)CheckBox-Generate a checkBox control.
d)Choice-Create a pop-up list.
